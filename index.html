Derm-LLaVA

AI-Powered Dermatological Assistant

Authors: Aranya Saha, Tanvir Ahmed Khan, Ismam Nur Swapnil, Mohammad Ariful Haque
Dept. of EEE, BUET

Paper Code Dataset

Summary

82.05% diagnostic accuracy with an 8/10 patient interaction score.

Derm-LLaVA combines vision-language models (LLaVA), DINOv2, and LLaMA to bring expert-level skin diagnosis to underserved populations globally.
