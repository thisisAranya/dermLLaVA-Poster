Abstract

Over 2.6 billion people worldwide lack access to specialized dermatological care, leading to preventable suffering and advanced disease progression in underserved communities. We present Derm-LLaVA, an intelligent dermatological assistant that bridges this critical healthcare gap through advanced multimodal AI.

Our system combines cutting-edge vision-language models with specialized medical training to deliver expert-level diagnostic capabilities. Through a sophisticated 4-stage training pipeline incorporating Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO), Direct Policy Optimization (DPO), and intelligent model compression, we achieve remarkable clinical performance while maintaining deployment efficiency.

Derm-LLaVA demonstrates 82.05% diagnostic accuracy compared to a 30.77% baseline, with an 8/10 patient interaction score. Our framework integrates LLaVA for visual question answering, DINOv2 for disease classification, and LLaMA for medical knowledge retrieval, creating the first multimodal medical assistant specifically optimized for dermatological applications.

AI Framework

We developed a sophisticated multimodal intelligence system combining cutting-edge AI techniques for clinical-grade dermatological diagnosis.

Key Components:

  • LLaVA: Vision-language model for medical image analysis
  • DINOv2: Vision transformer for disease classification
  • LLaMA: Knowledge retrieval and medical reasoning
  • Compression: Structured pruning for efficient deployment
